Our Story

Over a decade in education. A school built on the belief that every child deserves to be known.

Mission

Better One's Life

Edusky Private School is more than just a school; it is a community. We believe in fostering strong relationships between students, teachers and parents. Our dedicated faculty is committed to providing a nurturing environment where every child feels valued and inspired. We offer quality educational services to learners of all age groups and backgrounds, empowering them with the knowledge and personal skills necessary to shape their future.

Vision

Empowering Tomorrow's Leaders

We strive to accomplish academic excellence and a higher standard of living for every student. Our goal is delivering a holistic education that combines strong academics with deep character development, preparing students for success in personal and professional life.

Founder & Principal

Dr. Ng Peng Long

Founder & Director

Dr. Ng Peng Long holds a PhD in Marketing from Universiti Sains Malaysia and brings more than two decades of senior leadership in education and training. He spent seventeen years as a Director at Wawasan Open University, served as Deputy CEO of Austin Legacy Education (Asia Metropolitan University), and is currently CEO of Lead Future Holdings Sdn Bhd, where he leads work in talent development, training, certification, and ESG. Under his leadership, Edusky was built on a simple belief: that small classes, strong relationships, and a parent's ability to walk in unannounced and be greeted by name are not luxuries — they are the foundation of how children learn best. His vision for the school is rooted in collaboration, inclusivity, and academic excellence that prepares students to thrive in life, not just in exams.

Our Principles

What we stand for

★

Known by Name

No child falls through the cracks. Our teachers know each student's strengths, challenges, interests, and learning style — personally, not from a file.

♥

Nurtured to Grow

Every child feels valued and inspired. Warmth with boundaries. Clear expectations with consistent follow-through. Relationships strong enough to hold accountability and gentle enough to build trust.

🌱

Empowered for Life

We teach children how to think, not what to think. Confidence, creativity, and the ability to ask better questions. Academic excellence paired with character development prepares students to shape their future.

🤝

Rooted in Community

Strong relationships between students, teachers, and parents. CSR projects, parent workshops, expert talks, and student volunteering — a community built together, not an institution run from above.

Our Journey

From One Classroom to a Community

2014

Foundation

Edusky Learning Academy founded. A single classroom with a conviction: every child deserves to be known.

2016

Expansion

Expanded to multi-standard primary. First cohort of Standard 1–6 students.

2019

Upgrade

Facilities upgraded. Chinese investor delegation visits and positively assesses campus.

2022

Registration

Rebranded as Edusky Private School. SMIPS registration JDA1001. MOE-registered KSSR curriculum.

2026

Community

Micro-school model formalised. Community programmes, parent network, and alumni outreach begin.

Edusky Private School

An intentional micro-school in Taman Adda Height, Johor Bahru. Small by design. Big on heart.
