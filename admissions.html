FAQ

Questions parents ask

What curriculum does Edusky follow?

We follow the KSSR (Kurikulum Standard Sekolah Rendah), Malaysia's national primary curriculum approved by the Ministry of Education. Our SMIPS registration code is JDA1001.

What are the school hours?

School runs Mon–Thu 8:30 AM – 1:00 PM with Enrichment 2:00 PM – 4:00 PM, and Fri 8:30 AM – 12:00 PM.

What is the maximum class size?

We maintain a maximum teacher-student ratio of approximately 1:15. This is core to our philosophy — every child is known individually, taught personally, and supported fully.

Does Edusky accept children who need extra support?

Yes. We are an intentionally small school, which means teachers have the time and space to understand each child individually. Children who learn at a different pace often thrive here.

What ages does Edusky accept?

Standard 1 through Standard 6, typically ages 7 to 12.

Is there a trial period?

Yes. We offer a trial week — your child attends real classes before you make any long-term commitment.

How do I pay fees?

Fees can be paid by bank transfer, cheque, or online banking. We issue official receipts for all payments.

Is there after-school care?

Enrichment programmes and after-school tuition are available on-site for children who stay beyond regular hours.
