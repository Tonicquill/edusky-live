Facilities

Purpose-built spaces designed for small-group learning, hands-on discovery, and community gathering.

Spaces

A Real School With Real Spaces

Our campus at 71, Jalan Adda 3/1 includes purpose-built facilities visited and positively assessed by Chinese investors.

Computer Lab

Modern computing facilities for digital literacy and ICT lessons. Students build confidence with technology from an early age.

Science Lab

Hands-on experiments and inquiry-based learning in an equipped lab. Students learn by doing — not just watching.

Discussion Room

Small-group spaces for collaborative learning and peer discussion. Where ideas are shared and confidence is built.

Hall

Multi-purpose space for assemblies, performances, and gatherings. Concerts, celebrations, and community events happen here.

Library

Reading resources and quiet study space for all students. A place to explore, imagine, and discover independently.

Main Lobby

Welcoming entrance and reception area for parents and visitors. First impressions matter — ours is warm and intentional.

Classroom

Bright, air-conditioned spaces designed for small-group learning. Every child sits close enough to ask a question.

Surau

Prayer space supporting spiritual development and religious observance. Respect and inclusion woven into daily life.

Outdoor Space

Open-air area for physical activity, play, and outdoor learning. Fresh air and movement between lessons.

Edusky Private School

An intentional micro-school in Taman Adda Height, Johor Bahru. Small by design. Big on heart.
